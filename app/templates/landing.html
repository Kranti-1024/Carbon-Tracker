Why it matters

The average person emits about 12.9 kg CO2e a day. A 2050-aligned path needs roughly 5.5 kg.

Closing that gap isn't about guilt — it's about visibility. Most people have never seen their own number, let alone which single habit is driving it. Carbon Ledger exists to close that gap.
